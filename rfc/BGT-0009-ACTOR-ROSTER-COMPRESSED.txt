ACTOR ROSTER: 42 STAKEHOLDER TYPES
================================================================================

Each actor listed with ID, Name, and one-line relationship to money.

Optimizing for: Control over money and people
================================================================================

----  -------------------------  --------------------------------------------------------

================================================================================
COVERAGE PROOF
================================================================================

Total Actors: 42

Distinctness Test:
- Pw1-Pw9: 9 unique positions in money-control hierarchy
- Pr1-Pr11: 11 different revenue mechanisms
- Ps1-Ps11: 11 different wealth-erosion vectors
- Pd1-Pd11: 11 different resource conversions

Any unmodeled actor either:
(a) Maps to existing actor's incentive structure (same defense applies), or
(b) Lacks sufficient influence to alter equilibrium outcome.

To contest roster, specify: (1) which group the missing actor belongs to,
(2) why its money interface differs from all existing actors in that group,
and (3) which axiom (AX1-AX4) its inclusion would contradict.
